About Us

Adolph T. Resources Nigeria Limited is an indigenous company providing services to commercial, industrial, and domestic customers.

The company was established in 2022 and has provided scaffolding solutions to the building and maintenance industry throughout Nigeria. Today Adolph T. Resources Limited, Scaffolding Company is recognized as one of the leading scaffolding contractors in the region.

Our team carries out all categories of scaffolding operations, scaffolding trainings, including installation of safehouse pressurized habitat on a regular basis with many local authorities, as well as commercial clients such as oil companies and building contractors.
