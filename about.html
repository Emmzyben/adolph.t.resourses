About Us

The company was established in June 2021 and has provided scaffolding solutions to the building and maintenance industry throughout Nigeria.

Today Adolph T. Resources Limited,Scaffolding Company is recognized as one of the leading scaffolding contractors in the region.

We are supplying scaffolding services for large scale commercial, industrial, and domestic projects.

Our competences range from straight forward house building to the most complex designed scaffold structures.

Whatever your requirement, we can develop and deliver a scaffold to suit the work scope.

Our CISRS accredited scaffolders have experience of working with all scaffold types across many different environments.

Our team are experienced in building safehouse pressurized habitats for hot works in live plans.
