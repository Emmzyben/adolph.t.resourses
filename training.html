Scaffolding training


Our scaffolding course is a 10-days course for candidates who are working towards being a professional scaffolder in the industries. The training involves working as a team to safely erect, modify, and dismantle scaffolding structures.

We also carry out 2-days scaffolding refresher training for scaffolders who are already trained and experienced in the erection, modification, and dismantling of complex scaffold structures.
